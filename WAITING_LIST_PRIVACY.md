## 1. Personal Data We Collect

## 2. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

Your email address is processed solely to manage your participation in the Meishi Alpha Waiting List and to send you the following transactional notifications:

	•	Confirmation if you are among the first 1,000 sign-ups eligible for one year of free Premium access.
	•	Notification of alpha version availability.
	•	Notification of beta version availability.
	•	Notification of full public release on app stores (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play).

Legal basis for processing:

	•	Article 6(1)(b) GDPR – processing is necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.
	•	Article 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interest in managing access to the Meishi alpha program and informing early adopters.

Your email address is not used for marketing, profiling, advertising, or unrelated communications.

----

## 3. Data Retention

Your email address is retained only until the earlier of:

	•	the final notification regarding the public release of the Meishi app, or
	•	a maximum period of 2 years from the date of sign-up.

After this period, your email address is securely deleted, unless retention is required by law or you explicitly request otherwise.
